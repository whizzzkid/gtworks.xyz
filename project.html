ABOUT ME
Hello everyone, I am Garima Tuteja. I have graduated in B.Com (Hons) and I am currently pursuing Communication Design from Pearl Academy. I have always been deeply inclined towards creativity and I am a firm believer that
The desire to create is one of the deepest yearnings of the human soul. Also, one can mimic the result but not the creativity and holding my ground on this belief, I decided to share my design ideas and invested them to help school and college students with their projects.
I have gained myself a steady experience of more than 2 years and have slowly and steadily increased my line of business to bring you more creativity.
I assure you that all my ideas are 100% original, the products are handmade with delicacy and created and assembled with pure dedication and to the best of my efforts.
